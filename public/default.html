What is ShipBro

ShipBro is a web based unified messaging and networking system for maritime shipping business which allows the whole industry to be in touch with each other in a way with the highest privacy and complete control over their own contacts. We believe it is vital for maritime shipping business participator to protect their contacts from competitors. We designed the whole system based on this main principle. You don’t share your contacts but you communicate with your own contacts if you decide to invite them to your contact list trough their email address and after that it is up to you to let them see which users they can see in your contacts or you can ban their access to your contacts list altogether. Unlike the other professional social networks ShipBro is not aiming to connect people you don’t know or vice versa, althoug if you want you can. It is entirely under your control.
